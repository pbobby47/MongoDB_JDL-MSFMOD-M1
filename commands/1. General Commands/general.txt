>>> mongod --version:
    To know the mongoDB version

>>> mongosh
    To connect to mongo server.

>>> ctrl+c | .exit: 
    To disconnect from mongo server.

>>> cls | console.clear() : 
    To clear the screen.

>>> single tab:
    It will autofill the data.

>>> double tab:
    To know possible words / keywords

>>> cd folderName:
    To enter into child folder

>>> cd .. :
    To move back to parent folder.
